    Mobility User Guide

    Complete guide to using the Digital Mobility Platform for booking rides, managing your account, and more.

    Getting Started

    First Time Setup

    When you first open the Mobility app:

    1. Splash Screen - You'll see an animated splash screen with the Mobility logo
    2. Onboarding - A 3-slide carousel introduces you to the app's key features
    3. Welcome and app introduction
    4. Feature highlights
    5. How to get started
    6. Skip Option - You can skip the onboarding by tapping "Skip" if you prefer

    Creating an Account

    Sign Up

    1. On the authentication screen, tap the Sign Up tab
    2. Enter your email address
    3. Create a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
    4. Tap Create Account
    5. Check your email for verification (if required)

    Sign In

    1. On the authentication screen, tap the Sign In tab
    2. Enter your registered email
    3. Enter your password
    4. Tap Sign In

    Social Login (Coming Soon)

    Quick sign-in options with Google, Apple, or Facebook will be available soon.

    Home Screen

    The home screen is your command center for booking rides.

    Main Components

    • Interactive Map - Shows your current location and nearby area
    • Location Search - Search for pickup and destination addresses
    • Your Location - Displays your current address
    • Menu Drawer - Access all app features via the hamburger menu (☰)

    Granting Permissions

    For the app to work properly, you'll need to grant:

    • Location Permission - Required for showing your location and booking rides
    • Tap "Allow" when prompted
    • Choose "While Using the App" or "Always" (for background tracking)

    Booking a Ride

    Step 1: Set Pickup Location

    Your current location is automatically set as the pickup point, but you can change it:

    Option A: Search 1. Tap the "Where from?" search field 2. Type the pickup address 3. Select from the autocomplete suggestions 4. The map will center on the selected location

    Option B: Map Selection 1. Long-press on any location on the map 2. The pickup marker will move to that location 3. The address will be reverse-geocoded and displayed

    Option C: Saved Addresses 1. Tap the "Where from?" field 2. Select from "Home" or "Work" if you've saved these addresses 3. Or choose from your favorite locations

    Step 2: Set Destination

    1. Tap the "Where to?" search field
    2. Search for your destination using any of the methods above
    3. A route will be drawn on the map showing your path

    Step 3: Choose Ride Type

    Select the type of vehicle you prefer:

    Type Description Best For
    Economy Budget-friendly rides Solo travelers, short trips
    Comfort Mid-tier comfort Comfortable rides, medium trips
    Premium Luxury vehicles Special occasions, business
    XL Larger vehicles Groups, extra luggage

    Each option shows: - Estimated fare - Estimated arrival time (ETA) - Vehicle capacity

    Step 4: Review and Confirm

    1. Review the ride details:
    2. Pickup location
    3. Destination
    4. Estimated distance
    5. Estimated duration
    6. Fare estimate
    7. Choose your payment method
    8. Tap Request Ride or Book Now

    Scheduled Rides

    To book a ride for later:

    1. Tap the clock icon or "Schedule" option
    2. Select date and time
    3. Complete the booking as normal
    4. You'll receive a notification before your scheduled ride

    During Your Ride

    Finding Your Driver

    After requesting a ride:

    1. Driver Assigned - You'll see driver details:
    2. Driver name and photo
    3. Vehicle make, model, and color
    4. License plate number
    5. Driver rating

    6. Estimated arrival time

    7. Track Driver - Watch your driver's location in real-time on the map

    8. Contact Driver

    9. Tap the phone icon to call
    10. Tap the message icon to send a text (if supported)

    Driver Arrival

    When your driver arrives:

    1. You'll receive a notification
    2. The driver status will show "Arrived"
    3. Look for the vehicle matching the details shown
    4. Verify the license plate before entering

    Ride in Progress

    During your ride:

    • Live Tracking - Follow your route on the map
    • ETA Updates - See estimated time to destination
    • Share Trip - Tap "Share Ride" to send details to friends/family
    • Emergency - Access emergency button if needed (see Safety Features)

    Ride Completion

    When you arrive:

    1. The ride automatically ends
    2. Your payment is processed
    3. You'll see the final fare breakdown
    4. Rate your driver (1-5 stars)
    5. Optionally leave feedback

    Managing Saved Addresses

    Adding Addresses

    1. Open the side menu (☰)
    2. Tap Addresses or Saved Places
    3. Tap Add New Address
    4. Choose address type:
    5. Home
    6. Work
    7. Custom (give it a name)
    8. Enter or select the address
    9. Tap Save

    Editing Addresses

    1. Go to Addresses
    2. Tap on the address you want to edit
    3. Make your changes
    4. Tap Update

    Deleting Addresses

    1. Go to Addresses
    2. Swipe left on the address (iOS) or long-press (Android)
    3. Tap Delete
    4. Confirm deletion

    Payment Methods

    Adding a Payment Method

    1. Open the side menu
    2. Tap Payment Methods
    3. Tap Add Payment Method
    4. Choose payment type:
    5. Credit/Debit Card
    6. Mobile Wallet (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
    7. Cash
    8. Enter payment details
    9. Tap Save

    Setting Default Payment

    1. Go to Payment Methods
    2. Tap on your preferred method
    3. Toggle Set as Default
    4. This method will be automatically selected for new rides

    Managing Payment Methods

    • Edit - Update card details or expiration dates
    • Delete - Remove payment methods you no longer use
    • Set Default - Choose which method to use by default

    Payment for Rides

    Payment is processed automatically when the ride ends:

    1. The fare is charged to your default payment method
    2. You'll receive a receipt via email
    3. View payment details in Ride History

    Ride History

    Viewing Your Trips

    1. Open the side menu
    2. Tap History or My Rides
    3. See all your past trips in chronological order

    Trip Details

    Tap on any trip to view:

    • Route Map - Visual map of the trip
    • Addresses - Pickup and dropoff locations
    • Date & Time - When the trip occurred
    • Fare Breakdown:
    • Base fare
    • Distance charge
    • Time charge
    • Surge pricing (if applicable)
    • Taxes and fees
    • Total paid
    • Driver Info - Driver who completed the trip
    • Payment Method - How you paid

    Downloading Receipts

    1. Open a trip from history
    2. Tap Receipt or Download Receipt
    3. Choose to:
    4. Download PDF
    5. Email to yourself
    6. Share with others

    Filtering History

    • Search - Find trips by location or date
    • Date Range - Filter by specific time periods
    • Status - View completed, cancelled, or all trips

    Profile Management

    Viewing Your Profile

    1. Open the side menu
    2. Tap Profile or your name/photo at the top

    Editing Profile Information

    1. Go to your profile
    2. Tap Edit Profile
    3. Update any of the following:
    4. Full name
    5. Phone number
    6. Profile photo
    7. Tap Save Changes

    Updating Profile Photo

    1. In Edit Profile, tap the profile photo
    2. Choose:
    3. Take Photo - Use camera
    4. Choose from Library - Select existing photo
    5. Crop and adjust as needed
    6. Save changes

    Changing Email

    1. Go to Settings from the side menu
    2. Tap Account Settings
    3. Select Change Email
    4. Enter new email address
    5. Verify via email confirmation

    Changing Password

    1. Go to Settings
    2. Tap Account Settings
    3. Select Change Password
    4. Enter current password
    5. Enter new password
    6. Confirm new password
    7. Tap Update Password

    Safety Features

    Share Trip

    Share your live ride details with trusted contacts:

    1. During a ride, tap Share Trip
    2. Choose contact(s) to share with
    3. They'll receive a link to track your ride in real-time
    4. Sharing ends when the ride completes

    Emergency Contacts

    Add emergency contacts for quick access:

    1. Go to Safety in the side menu
    2. Tap Emergency Contacts
    3. Tap Add Contact
    4. Select from your phone contacts or enter manually
    5. Save

    Emergency Button

    If you feel unsafe during a ride:

    1. Tap the Emergency button (shield icon)
    2. Options:
    3. Call Emergency Services (911/local emergency number)
    4. Share Location with emergency contact
    5. Report Issue to Mobility support

    Safety Center

    Access safety resources and tips:

    1. Go to Safety in the side menu
    2. Browse:
    3. Safety tips
    4. Community guidelines
    5. Driver verification info
    6. Insurance coverage details

    Ratings and Feedback

    Rating Your Driver

    After each ride:

    1. You'll see a rating screen
    2. Tap 1-5 stars (5 being excellent)
    3. Optionally select feedback categories:
    4. Great conversation
    5. Clean vehicle
    6. Safe driving
    7. Smooth ride
    8. Add written comments (optional)
    9. Tap Submit

    Your Passenger Rating

    Drivers also rate passengers:

    1. View your rating in Profile
    2. Your rating is the average of all your trip ratings
    3. Maintain a good rating by:
    4. Being respectful
    5. Being ready at pickup time
    6. Not eating/drinking without asking
    7. Not leaving trash

    Reporting Issues

    If you experienced a problem:

    1. Go to History
    2. Select the trip
    3. Tap Report Issue
    4. Choose issue type:
    5. Driver behavior
    6. Route problem
    7. Pricing issue
    8. Lost item
    9. Safety concern
    10. Provide details
    11. Submit report

    Settings

    App Settings

    1. Open the side menu
    2. Tap Settings

    General Settings

    • Language - Choose your preferred language
    • Units - Miles or kilometers
    • Currency - Display currency preference

    Notifications

    Control what notifications you receive:

    • Ride Updates - Driver assigned, arrival, completion
    • Promotions - Special offers and discounts
    • Payment Notifications - Payment confirmations
    • General Announcements - App updates and news

    Turn each on/off individually.

    Privacy Settings

    • Location Services - Manage location permissions
    • Data Sharing - Control analytics and data collection
    • Profile Visibility - Control what drivers see

    Appearance

    • Theme - Light, Dark, or Automatic (follows system)
    • Map Style - Choose map appearance

    Accessibility

    • Text Size - Adjust font size
    • Screen Reader - Enable/disable
    • High Contrast - Improve visibility

    Promotions and Discounts

    Viewing Available Offers

    1. Go to Promotions in the side menu
    2. See all active promotions:
    3. Discount percentage or amount
    4. Expiration date
    5. Terms and conditions

    Applying Promo Codes

    1. When booking a ride, tap Promo Code
    2. Enter the code
    3. Tap Apply
    4. Discount will be reflected in the fare estimate

    Earning Credits

    You may earn ride credits through:

    • Referral program (invite friends)
    • Promotional campaigns
    • Loyalty rewards

    View your credit balance in Profile → Wallet.

    Referral Program

    Inviting Friends

    1. Go to Referrals in the side menu
    2. Tap Invite Friends
    3. Share your unique referral code via:
    4. Text message
    5. Email
    6. Social media
    7. Copy link

    Earning Rewards

    When friends sign up with your code:

    • They get a welcome bonus
    • You earn credits after their first ride
    • Track your referrals and rewards in the Referrals section

    Troubleshooting

    App Issues

    Can't Sign In

    • Verify email and password are correct
    • Try Forgot Password to reset
    • Check internet connection
    • Update app to latest version

    Location Not Working

    • Enable Location Services in device settings
    • Grant location permission to Mobility app
    • Restart the app
    • Check GPS signal

    Map Not Loading

    • Check internet connection
    • Verify Mapbox is not experiencing outages
    • Clear app cache (Settings → Clear Cache)
    • Reinstall the app if problem persists

    Payment Failed

    • Verify payment method is valid and not expired
    • Check sufficient funds/credit limit
    • Try a different payment method
    • Contact your bank if card is declined

    Ride Issues

    Can't Find Driver

    • Check the vehicle details (make, model, color, plate)
    • Call or message the driver
    • Check the pin location is accurate
    • Wait at the designated pickup spot

    Driver Cancelled

    • You'll be automatically matched with another driver
    • No cancellation fee if driver cancels
    • Contact support if this happens repeatedly

    Wrong Fare Charged

    • Review fare breakdown in trip details
    • Check for surge pricing
    • Report issue through the trip in History
    • Support will review and refund if necessary

    Getting Help

    • In-App Support - Side menu → Support
    • Email - support@mobility.com
    • Phone - Available in Support section
    • Help Center - Browse FAQs and guides

    Tips for Best Experience

    For Riders

    1. Set Exact Pickup Location - Be specific to avoid confusion
    2. Be Ready - Be at pickup location when driver arrives
    3. Communicate - Message driver if you're running late
    4. Verify Vehicle - Check license plate before entering
    5. Buckle Up - Always wear your seatbelt
    6. Be Respectful - Treat drivers with courtesy
    7. Rate Honestly - Help improve the community
    8. Save Favorite Locations - Makes booking faster
    9. Keep App Updated - Get latest features and fixes
    10. Share Trips - Share important rides with family

    Saving Money

    • Use promo codes when available
    • Book during off-peak hours to avoid surge pricing
    • Choose Economy for budget-friendly rides
    • Share rides with friends (when feature is available)
    • Refer friends to earn credits
