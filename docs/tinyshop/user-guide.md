# TinyShop User Guide

Complete guide to managing your online store with TinyShop.

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides a quick overview of your store:

- Recent orders
- Sales statistics
- Popular products
- Low stock alerts

## Product Management

### Adding Products

To add a new product:

1. Go to Products > Add New
2. Enter product details
3. Upload product images
4. Set pricing and inventory
5. Click "Save"

### Product Categories

Organize products with categories:

- Create hierarchical categories
- Assign products to multiple categories
- Set category images and descriptions

### Inventory Management

Track your stock levels:

- Set stock quantities
- Enable low stock alerts
- Manage product variants

## Order Management

### Processing Orders

Handle customer orders efficiently:

1. View new orders in the Orders section
2. Update order status (Processing, Shipped, Completed)
3. Print invoices and packing slips
4. Send status notifications to customers

### Order Statuses

- **Pending** - Awaiting payment
- **Processing** - Payment received, preparing shipment
- **Shipped** - Order dispatched
- **Completed** - Order delivered
- **Cancelled** - Order cancelled
