# Mobility Installation

Learn how to install and deploy the Mobility platform for your organization.

## System Requirements

### iOS Requirements

- iOS 14.0 or later
- Compatible with iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch

### Android Requirements

- Android 8.0 (API level 26) or higher
- Google Play Services

## Installation Methods

### App Store Installation

#### iOS

1. Open the App Store
2. Search for "Thinesoft Mobility"
3. Tap "Get" to download and install

#### Android

1. Open Google Play Store
2. Search for "Thinesoft Mobility"
3. Tap "Install"

### Enterprise Distribution

For enterprise deployment, contact your IT administrator or see our [enterprise deployment guide](configuration.md#enterprise-deployment).

## Initial Configuration

After installation, launch the app and:

1. Enter your organization's server URL
2. Log in with your credentials
3. Complete the onboarding process

## Verification

Verify your installation by checking:

- App launches successfully
- You can log in
- Data syncs properly

## Troubleshooting

Common installation issues and solutions:

- **Can't find the app** - Ensure your device meets the minimum requirements
- **Login fails** - Verify your server URL and credentials
- **Sync issues** - Check your internet connection
